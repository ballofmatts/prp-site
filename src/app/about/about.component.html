100% Cream, 100% Dream

Party Roll is a Dungeons and Dragons Actual Play podcast started by a bunch of friends in late 2014.

The group had been playing Pathfinder and D&D for several years before they had the collective idea of: "What if we could record these Cory-giggles and share them with the world?"

The Result is this! Mark, Steven, Cory, Dusty, Rory, Matt, Vanessa, and Jose have since spent their time having fun gathering around the table and exploring the various worlds created by our DMs. A lot has changed since we started this podcast, but D&D never dies.
